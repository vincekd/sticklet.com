Help

Tips

  • Double-click to create a new sticklet at the mouse position.
  • Visit the archive menu to permanently delete or restore a sticklet.
  • Offline? You can move, color, and read your sticklets in the browser! No changes made when offline are saved and will be changed back when next online. Too access your sticklets when you're offline, open your browser and navigate the page as you normally would.
  • Share your sticklet with others by clicking the share button and typing in the email address.

Shortcuts

ctrl+z:undo
ctrl+y:redo
shift+?:help menu
esc:save/close sticklet
shift-a:Open archive menu
